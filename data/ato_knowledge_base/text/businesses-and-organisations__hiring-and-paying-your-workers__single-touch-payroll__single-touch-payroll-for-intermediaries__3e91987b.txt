Last updated 1 October 2019 Print or Download If you are a registered agent providing a payroll service, you will need to use Single Touch Payroll (STP)-enabled software to report for your clients. If you are an insolvency practitioner for an 'incapacitated entity' under administration, you will need to work out how the entity's STP obligations can be met.
