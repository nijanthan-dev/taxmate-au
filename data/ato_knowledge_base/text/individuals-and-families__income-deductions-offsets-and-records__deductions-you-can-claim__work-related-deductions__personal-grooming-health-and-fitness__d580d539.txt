Medical assessments and vaccinations Deductions you can and can't claim for medical expenses, dental expenses, vaccinations and COVID-19 test expenses. Personal appearance and grooming As a rule, you can't claim the costs of personal grooming or cosmetics. Gym fees and fitness-related expenses Except in very limited circumstances, you can't claim gym fees or fitness-related expenses.
