Last updated 11 May 2026 Print or Download Our guides Our occupation and industry guides help you to complete your tax return. Use these guides to: work out what income and allowances to declare check if you can or can't claim work-related expenses as a deduction find out what records you need to keep. To claim a deduction for work-related expenses, you must meet the 3 golden rules: You must have spent the money and you weren't reimbursed. The expense must directly relate to earning your income. You must keep records that show you incur the expense (usually a receipt). Occupation and industry guides Tailored occupation and industry specific guides help you get your tax return right.
